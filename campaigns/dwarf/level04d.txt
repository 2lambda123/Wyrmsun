As the gates closed, you went to your clan's central underground settlement, to convene with your elders.

Durstorn, the clan's chief, angry that you didn't manage to negotiate a better price with the Gnomish king, has placed on your shoulders the burden of finding Thursagan, one of the few runemasters who would be able to craft a scepter as the king wishes.

Your underground journey to the far northern wastelands, where Thursagan's home lays, was long and perilous and filled with goblins, but now you are close to finding the sage of fire...
